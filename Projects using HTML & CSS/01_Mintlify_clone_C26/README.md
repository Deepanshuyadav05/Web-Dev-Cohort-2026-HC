## Sections-recreated

**Intelligent Navigation:** A navbar featuring dropdown-ready and glassMorphism effect, resource links and distinct primary/secondary CTA buttons.
* **Hero Section:** A dynamic header with a glass-effect background, email lead capture, and the iconic Mintlify dark-themed dashboard illustration with a background image.
* **Trust Bar:** A grid layout featuring partner logos (Anthropic, Coinbase, Perplexity, etc.).
* **AI-Native Features:** Three-part showcase featuring embedded video loops for "LLMs.txt & MCP," "Context-Aware Agent," and "Intelligent Assistant."
* **Enterprise Reinvention:** A dedicated section highlighting security, partnership, and SOC 2/GDPR compliance features.
* **Customer Success Cards:** A card-based layout featuring case studies from Perplexity, X, and Kalshi.
* **Call-to-Action & Footer:** A dual-card pricing/quickstart section leading into a robust, multi-column sitemap.

## üé® Design Specifications

### Fonts
The project utilizes a "Inter", sans-serif typography stack to mimic the **Inter** and **System UI** aesthetic of the original site.

* **Headings:** Bold weights with tight letter-spacing.
* **Body:** High-legibility spacing with a focus on a "Dark Mode" aesthetic.

### Colors & Styles
* website-hero-mint-color: `#18E299`
* Text-color: `#ffffff`
* Grey-border-color: `rgba(255,255,255,0.05)`
* bg-color: `#141516`
* place-holder-color: `#6c757d`
